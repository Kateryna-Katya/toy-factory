About the Game

Match. Sort. Party.

In Toy Factory: 3D Match Puzzle, players match identical 3D toys in a colorful toy factory setting. Your task? Sort through stacked puzzles and clear all toy sets before time runs out. With fresh toy types each level, the challenge never ends!

  • Perfect for fans of match 3, sort, and tile games

  • Quick play sessions or deep matching marathons

  • Designed for fun, crafted for puzzle-lovers

How to Play

Clear the Board in 3 Simple Steps

Tap and drag 3 identical toys to match them.
Tip: Use rotation and zoom to find hidden matches!

  • 1

    Use boosters to get through tough setups

  • 2

    Solve puzzles before time runs out to move to the next stage

  • 3

    Keep your toy slots clear or it’s game over!

  • 4

    Unlock new rooms in the toy factory as you progress
