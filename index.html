About the Game

Match. Sort. Party.

In Toy Factory: 3D Match Puzzle, players match identical 3D toys in a colorful toy factory setting. Your task? Sort through stacked puzzles and clear all toy sets before time runs out. With fresh toy types each level, the challenge never ends!
